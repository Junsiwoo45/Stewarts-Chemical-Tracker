Tree Service!

The Tree service at Stewart's is a specialist service that involves arborist work.
From Spraying trees and shrubs for insects or injecting them for fertilizer. Depending on what the tree
needs is what kind of service they need. For example, sometimes furs and pines get something like scale
or bark beetle. Those need to be sprayed to prevent the damage from those two insects from happening.
Or you could have aphid issue, we would spray the leafs and the foliage for that to kill those insects
before they cause damage to the tree or shrub. With injection's we drill holes in the tree and shoot
chemical into the IV system of the tree. It's usually an insecticide or Iron(fertilizer). We also do
General Tree and Shrub fertilizer that keep everything from flowers to trees to be healthy. A granual
is applied in the flowerbeds, then if there is any tree outside of those they are injected at the root
ball of the tree.
